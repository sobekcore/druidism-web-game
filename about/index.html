Druidism is a 16-bit platformer game made with HTML and Vanilla JS 🍦. With the help of The Great Almighty Internet i've managed to create this game, and make it playable. In this game you are a druid (who would guess it lol) who is trying to finish his path in shorthest time possible, also picking as many mushrooms as possible.
